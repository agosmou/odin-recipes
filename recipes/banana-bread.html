Banana Bread Recipe

How to make a healthy-ish Banana Bread

  • Prep: 15 mins
  • Servings: 12
  • Cook: 1 hr 5 mins
  • Yield: 1 loaf
  • Cooking Time: 1 hr 20 mins

Ingredients List for 12 Servings

  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/2 cups of mashed overripe bananas

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 350F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9" x 5" inch loaf pan
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and baking soda. In a separate bowl pour in melted butter. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
